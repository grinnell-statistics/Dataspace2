    • Instructions to obtain student data
  • To obtain student data on the survey questions for DataSpace, you first must create an account at this website using your email as your username (less than 40 characters) and you can choose any password you like (less than 20 characters). Please remember/write down this password to be used for the next step.
  • After successfully creating your account, you can then go to the data retrieving tool located here. Enter in your email and password and select which data you would like to download.
  • Please note that you will only retrieve data from students who enter YOUR EMAIL/USERNAME in the instructor email section for the surveys.
  • Please contact kuipers@grinnell.edu if you encounter any issues.
